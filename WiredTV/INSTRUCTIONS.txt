HOW TO GET YOUR OWN SERIES/ADS/BUMPERS
####### EPISODES #######
1.Go to Resources/Media
2.Add a folder for the series
3.Paste in all the episodes named "E1", "E2", "E3"... "En"
####### BUMPERS #######
1.Go to Resources/Bumpers
2.Paste in all the bumpers you want
####### ADS #######
1.Go to Resources/Ads
2.Paste in all the ads you want
